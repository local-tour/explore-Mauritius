Our Tours in Mauritius

Explore the beauty of Mauritius with our specially curated tours. Whether you seek thrilling adventures, serene beaches, or rich cultural experiences, we have it all!

Adventure Tour

Discover the untamed beauty of Mauritius through exhilarating activities like hiking, zip-lining, and rock climbing.

Hiking at Le Morne Brabant

Climb the majestic Le Morne Brabant mountain and enjoy the breathtaking views.

Zip-Lining Adventure

Feel the adrenaline rush while flying through the lush forests on a zipline.

Canyoning at Tamarind Falls

Experience the thrill of canyoning as you navigate the waterfalls and rocky terrain.

Beach Tour

Relax on the pristine beaches of Mauritius and soak in the sun and sea.

Flic en Flac Beach

Relax on the beautiful white sandy beaches and swim in the crystal-clear waters.

Blue Bay Marine Park

Snorkel in the Blue Bay Marine Park and explore the vibrant marine life.

Pereybere Beach

Enjoy the calm and picturesque surroundings of Pereybere Beach.

Cultural Tour

Immerse yourself in the rich history and culture of Mauritius through its landmarks and traditions.

Aapravasi Ghat

Discover the UNESCO World Heritage Site, Aapravasi Ghat, a place with deep historical significance.

Grand Bassin

Visit the sacred Grand Bassin, also known as Ganga Talao, a place of worship for Mauritians of Hindu faith.

Château de Labourdonnais

Explore the stunning Château de Labourdonnais, a historical mansion with a beautiful garden and museum.
